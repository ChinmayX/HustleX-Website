# Connect Your *Tracker* to HustleX

## Samsung Health

### Prerequisites

- The **Samsung Health** app installed. Install from [Galaxy Store](http://apps.samsung.com/appquery/appDetail.as?appId=com.sec.android.app.shealth) or [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.sec.android.app.shealth)
- The **Health Connect** app installed. Install from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata)

## How to Sync

- Open the **Samsung Health** app.

<Note>If you haven't set up Samsung Health before, you'll need to accept the terms, sign in, and complete your profile
	setup.</Note>

- Tap **My Page** (bottom right).
- Tap the **More** icon in the top right.
- Go to **Settings**.
- Scroll down and tap **Health Connect**.
- Select **App permissions** and find **Samsung Health**.
- Tap **Allow all** to give Samsung Health permission to write data to Health Connect.
- Tap **Allow**.

<Note>
	If you don't see Health Connect listed, make sure the app is installed and up to date.

	On some phones, the Health Connect app may not be visible in your app list, but it can still be accessed through the
	[Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata).
</Note>

- Open the **Health Connect** app.
- Tap **App permissions**.
- Select **Samsung Health** and confirm the following are enabled: **Steps**, **Distance**, **Active Calories Burned**, **Total Calories Burned**.
