# Connect Your *Tracker* to HustleX

## Polar Flow

### Prerequisites

- The **Polar Flow** app installed. Install from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=fi.polar.flow)
- The **Health Connect** app installed. Install from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata)

## How to Sync

- Open the **Polar Flow** app.

<Note>If you haven't set up Polar Flow before, you'll need to create an account or sign in, pair your device, and grant
	basic permissions.</Note>

- Tap the **Menu** icon (top left).
- Tap **General settings**.
- Under **Connect**, toggle on **Health Connect**.
- Tap **Allow all** to give Polar Flow permission to write data to Health Connect.
- Tap **Allow**.

<Note>
	If you don't see Health Connect listed, make sure the app is installed and up to date.

	On some phones, the Health Connect app may not be visible in your app list, but it can still be accessed through the
	[Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata).
</Note>

- Open the **Health Connect** app.
- Tap **App permissions**.
- Select **Polar Flow** and confirm the following are enabled: **Steps**, **Distance**, **Active Calories Burned**, **Total Calories Burned**.
