# Connect Your *Tracker* to HustleX

## Google Fit

### Prerequisites

- The **Google Fit** app installed. Install from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.fitness)
- The **Health Connect** app installed. Install from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata)

## How to Sync

- Open the **Google Fit** app.

<Note>If you haven't set up Google Fit before, you will need to create a Google account or sign in with an existing one.
	After signing in, follow the prompts to set up your profile and grant permissions.</Note>

- Tap your **Profile** tab (bottom right).
- Tap the **Settings** icon in the top right.
- Scroll down and toggle on **Sync Fit with Health Connect**.
- Tap **Allow all** to give Google Fit permission to write data to Health Connect.
- Tap **Allow**.

<Note>
	If you don't see Health Connect listed, make sure the app is installed and up to date.

	On some phones, the Health Connect app may not be visible in the app list, but it can still be accessed through the
	[Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata).
</Note>

- Open the **Health Connect** app.
- Tap **App permissions**.
- Select **Google Fit** and confirm the following are enabled: **Steps**, **Distance**, **Active Calories Burned**, **Total Calories Burned**.
