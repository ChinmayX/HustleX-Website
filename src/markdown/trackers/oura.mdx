# Connect Your *Tracker* to HustleX

## Oura

### Prerequisites

- The **Oura** app installed. Install from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ouraring.oura)
- The **Health Connect** app installed. Install from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata)

## How to Sync

- Open the **Oura** app.

<Note>If you haven't set up the Oura app before, you'll need to create an account or sign in, pair your ring, and grant
	basic permissions.</Note>

- Tap the **Menu** icon (top left).
- Tap **Settings**.
- Under **Data Sharing**, Tap **Health Connect**.
- Tap **Allow all** to give Oura permission to write data to Health Connect.
- Tap **Allow**.

<Note>
	If you don't see Health Connect listed, make sure the app is installed and up to date.

	On some phones, the Health Connect app may not be visible in your app list, but it can still be accessed through the
	[Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata).
</Note>

- Open the **Health Connect** app.
- Tap **App permissions**.
- Select **Oura** and confirm the following are enabled: **Steps**, **Distance**, **Active Calories Burned**, **Total Calories Burned**.
