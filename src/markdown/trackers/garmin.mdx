# Connect Your *Tracker* to HustleX

## Garmin

### Prerequisites

- The **Garmin Connect** app installed. Install from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.garmin.android.apps.connectmobile)
- The **Health Connect** app installed. Install from [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata)

## How to Sync

- Open the **Garmin Connect** app.

<Note>If you haven't set up Garmin Connect before, you'll need to create an account or sign in, set up your profile, and
	grant basic permissions.</Note>

- Tap the **More** tab (bottom right).
- Tap **Settings**.
- Tap **Connected Apps**.
- Tap **Health Connect**.
- Tap **Connect**.
- Tap **Allow all** to give Garmin Connect permission to write data to Health Connect.
- Tap **Allow**.

<Note>
	If you don't see Health Connect listed, make sure the app is installed and up to date.

	On some phones, the Health Connect app may not be visible in the app list, but it can still be accessed through the
	[Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata).
</Note>

- Open the **Health Connect** app.
- Tap **App permissions**.
- Select **Garmin Connect** and confirm the following are enabled: **Steps**, **Distance**, **Active Calories Burned**, **Total Calories Burned**.
