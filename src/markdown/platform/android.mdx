# Health Connect *(Android)*

## Connect Your Tracker to Health Connect

Health Connect lets you sync fitness data across multiple health and fitness apps. By connecting your tracker to Health Connect, you'll be able to sync steps, distance, and more with HustleX.

<HealthConnect />

## Prerequisites

- An Android phone running **Android 10 or later**.

<Note>To find your Android version, go to **Settings** > **About phone** > **Software information** > **Android
	version**.</Note>

- **Health Connect** app installed. You can download it from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.apps.healthdata).
- HustleX installed on your device. Download it [here](https://hustlex.club/download).

## How to Connect

- Open the **Health Connect** app.
- Tap **App permissions**.
- Find and select **HustleX** from the list of apps.

<Note>If HustleX isn't listed, make sure you've opened the app at least once after installing it.</Note>

- Tap **Allow all** to give HustleX permission to read your health data.
